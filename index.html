About Me

I've made my home base in Laredo, TX, where my family and I are practicing our español and enjoying the multicultural community. Before entering Tech, I worked for seven years as a Marriage & Family Therapist. In 2010 I built my first website for myself while beginning my therapy practice. I am also the author of a parenting book about technology, "Early Childhood Media Mastery: A Complete Guide to Teaching Safe and Responsible Media Use".

I enjoy learning about how people engage with technology. My goal is to create intuitive solutions to connect businesses or organizations with users on the internet. My focus is on front-end development, where I can use my organization skills and eye for detail to write clean, elegant code.
